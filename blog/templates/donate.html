SUPPORT THE ZEN CENTER

Chung Tai Zen Center of Houston offers classes, meditation retreats, workshops, and others programs at no charge as a service to the community. It is supported by the generous offerings (“dana”) from individuals. “Dana” is the first of the six perfections a bodhisattva practices; your offering will help sustain the Zen Center and help build future Chung Tai Zen Centers in the U.S.

Chung Tai International Chan Buddhist Association (of which the Zen Center of Sunnyvale is a branch) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit religious organization; your donations are fully tax deductible.
